Thailand

 In 2022 I took a wonderful trip to Thailand. A friend and I went through divorces around the same time, so we decided to take a divorcemoon instead of a honeymoon. We visited 5 different cities in 3 weeks. We got to bathe elephants, watch a muay thai fight, eat lots of delicious Thai food, and we even got matching bamboo tattoos. This trip was so cathartic for me after going through such a difficult divorce. Not only was it a great bonding experience for my friend and I, it also showed me that there is still more to do in this world. Even though I went through something dark, there is still so much light in this world to be seen!

Prague & Vienna

 In 2024 I took a trip to Prague and Vienna for my 39th birthday. I really wanted to do something big for my last year in my 30s, and this trip did not disappoint! One of my favorite things about this trip was all the art and architecture. In Vienna, I went to the Belvedere Museum that had several Gustav Klimt paintings, as well as one of only five oil paintings of the famous "Napoleon Crossing the Alps" portrait. In between Prague and Vienna, I stopped at the Sedlec Ossuary, which was one of my favorite sites. They don't allow photos, but this Ossuary houses the most beautiful sculptures and architecture made from over 40,000 skeletal bones. The place I stayed in Prague was right in the center of the Old Town Square, where the Astronomical Clock is. Last year the Czechia Ice Hockey team won the World Championship while I was there. They threw a big party in the center of Old Town Square and the whole team came through with their IIHF Championship trophy. It was such an incredible trip, and a great way to end my 30s!
